COMPLIANCE SOLUTIONS

FINEX provides comprehensive compliance solutions, helping your organization navigate complex regulatory landscapes and maintain adherence to industry standards.

Understanding the Regulatory Landscape

Financial cybersecurity regulations ensure the protection of sensitive data and financial stability worldwide.

Key Regulatory Standards

  • PCI DSS: Protects credit card data with strict security measures.
  • GDPR: Ensures data privacy for EU citizens globally.
  • GLBA: Protects consumers' financial information.
  • CCPA: California Consumer Privacy Act enhances consumer rights.
  • SOX: Sarbanes-Oxley Act enforces financial transparency.
